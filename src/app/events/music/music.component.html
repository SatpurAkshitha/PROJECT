Music event management is a multifaceted process that orchestrates the creation and execution of live music experiences. It encompasses a wide range of tasks and responsibilities, starting with conceptualization and culminating in the actual event. Event managers are responsible for everything from artist and venue selection to budgeting, marketing, logistics, and crowd control.

Music event management involves planning, organizing, and executing musical performances or festivals.

It includes tasks such as artist booking, venue selection, ticketing, marketing, and logistics.
